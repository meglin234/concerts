Transcription

Since there are a variety of music notation softwares available, I was able to accurately recreate my hand drawn visualization. When I began transcribing my visualization into the software I did not think I would be able to change the note's color, so I was planning on using bass clef for people I spoke to. Fortunately, I was able to reactivate my Flat account from highschool which allowed me to change the note's color.
